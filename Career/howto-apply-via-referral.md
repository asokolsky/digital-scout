# HOWTO Apply via Referral

Usually the referrer gets paid if the referred is hired.  If your application is already in the company database, the referrer is NOT paid.  This will significantly reduce their motivation to help you.  Therefore the sequence of the steps IS important:

* identify the position with the company where you have connection(s), get the requisitionID(s);
* contact your connection, express the interest in the opening, provide the requisitionID(s), your resume, and your strongest points you want to emphasize;
* ask your connection to file your resume as a referral to the specific requisitionID(s);
* for the extra credit, ask your connection to contact the hiring manager and put a good word out for you.

Benefits:
* you will stand out;
* your contact may help you in identifying the hiring manager;
* your contact may help you in getting feedback if your application is rejected;
* your contact now has a vested interest in your success.

Lessons to learn:
* you do NOT want for your resume to be filed with very company under the sun - this will take away the power of the referral;
* stay in control of your resume - every time you talk to any recruiter make sure to say: "please make sure to NOT submit my resume to any position without my prior approval".
